Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Gather 3 or more players, pick a vibe, and pass the phone. Each round one player is the Judge and picks a card type; everyone else answers, debates, or votes; the Judge crowns a favorite. Most points wins.

How many players do I need?

Three to twelve players on a single device.

What are the "Coming Soon" decks?

We're building more themed decks. They'll unlock in a future update — stay tuned.

Is there a cost?

Dazed & Amuzed is free to play with all current decks included.

What is Drinking Mode?

An optional 21+ setting that adds drinking-themed prompts. It's off by default. Please play responsibly and know your limits.
