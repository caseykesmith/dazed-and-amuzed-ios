## Description (4000)
```
Dazed & Amuzed is the party game that reads the room — and takes it from there.

Gather your friends, pass the phone, and let the chaos begin. Whether you want
to keep it light or go deep, Dazed & Amuzed serves up the perfect prompt for the
moment. No setup, no rules to memorize, no account required. Just pick a vibe and
play.

HOW IT WORKS
• 3–12 players on one phone
• Each round, one player is the Judge and picks a card type
• Everyone else answers, debates, or votes
• The Judge crowns a favorite — most points wins

PICK YOUR VIBE
• Party Mode — fast, funny, say-no-more energy
• Go Deep — the conversations you'll actually remember
• Best of Both — a little of everything

THE DECKS
• Debate — settle the arguments that divide the group
• Would You Rather — impossible choices, instant chaos
• Spill — the stories you've been dying to tell
• Unfiltered — honest, no-holds-barred prompts
• Exposed — find out what your friends really think
• Guilty — confessions, hot seats, and "drink if…"

Perfect for pregames, road trips, date nights, reunions, and any time the group
chat finally shows up in person.

Free to play with every current deck included. More themed decks coming soon.

Drinking Mode (optional, 21+) adds drinking-themed prompts — off by default.
Please play responsibly.
```
